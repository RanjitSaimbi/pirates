Tairan, Young Pirate

The very first Young Pirates of Langdon have travelled 1000 years in the future to tell you about the funny, amazing, scary and weird futures we might find. On this website you will find a mixture of letters from the future and descriptions of futuristic characters.

All our projects focus on the voice and perspective of our Young Pirates. We hope you enjoy this insight into their hopes, dreams and imaginings of the future. A salute to their hard work and to everyone who has supported them along the way.
